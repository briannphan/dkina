Adminstrator Panel

As the adminstrator, you will be able to control the digital Kina monetary policy, supply, and access.

Mint Digital Kina

Issue new digital Kina into the ecosystem. Choose an amount, and where to send it to.

Burn Digital Kina

Remove new digital Kina from the ecosystem. Choose an amount.

Blacklist

Deny usage of digital Kina from anyone in the ecosystem. Enter an address to blacklist.

Un-Blacklist

Enable usage of digital Kina from anyone in the ecosystem who has been blacklisted. Enter an address to un-blacklist.

Pause

Pause all activity and transactions related to digital Kina.

Unpause

Unpause to resume actiity regarding digital Kina.

Change Minter

Change the role of minter/adminstrator to someone else. Enter an address.
